About Me

I am a dedicated Senior Lecturer at the Faculty of Computer & Mathematical Sciences, Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Cawangan Pahang. With a PhD in Computer Science, my career spans across both robust academic research and practical industry software development.

I’m driven by a deep curiosity to transform emerging technologies into solutions that make a real difference—particularly in AI, IoT, and intelligent systems. My career bridges both industry and academia. I began as a software engineer and AI developer, building real-world systems across finance and technology sectors, before moving into academia as a Senior Lecturer at UiTM.

Today, I focus on teaching, mentoring, and leading research that connects theory with practice. My work spans machine learning, predictive analytics, and smart applications, with contributions including indexed publications, international innovation awards, and funded research projects such as FRGS.

I’m especially passionate about applying technology in areas like smart agriculture, sustainability, and assistive solutions. Moving forward, I aim to foster impactful collaborations, translate research into real-world applications, and inspire students to become future-ready innovators in the evolving digital landscape.
